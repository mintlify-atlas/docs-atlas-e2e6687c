# What is Biovity?

Biovity is a Chilean SaaS platform that specializes in connecting scientific talent with job opportunities across biotechnology, biochemistry, chemistry, chemical engineering, and health sectors. Unlike general job boards, Biovity focuses exclusively on the scientific community, providing a tailored experience for both job seekers and employers.

## Our mission

Biovity exists to **facilitate the connection between scientific talent and companies** in Chile's growing biotech and health sectors. We make it easier for professionals and students to find meaningful career opportunities while helping companies discover qualified candidates in specialized scientific fields.

<Note>
  Biovity means "Bio" (life sciences) + "vity" (activity/vitality) - representing the vibrant connection between scientific talent and opportunities.
</Note>

## Who Biovity serves

### Scientific professionals

Experienced researchers, lab technicians, quality analysts, and other science professionals looking for:

- Career advancement opportunities in their specialized field
- Transparent salary information specific to the scientific sector
- Direct connections with biotech and health companies
- A platform that understands scientific roles and requirements

<Tip>
  Professionals can create detailed profiles highlighting their scientific background, publications, certifications, and technical skills.
</Tip>

### Students and early-career scientists

University students and recent graduates in scientific disciplines seeking:

- Entry-level positions and internships in their field of study
- Practical experience in biotechnology, chemistry, or health sectors
- Career guidance and professional development resources
- Opportunities to apply their academic knowledge in real-world settings

### Scientific companies and organizations

Biotech firms, pharmaceutical companies, research labs, and health organizations that need:

- Access to qualified scientific talent with specific technical skills
- A streamlined hiring process for specialized roles
- Verified candidates with relevant scientific backgrounds
- Tools to manage job postings and applications efficiently

<Info>
  Companies get dedicated dashboards to post verified job offers, manage candidates, and track hiring metrics.
</Info>

## How Biovity works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Registration and profiles">
    Users register as either employees (job seekers) or organizations (employers). Each creates a profile with relevant information:
    
    - **Employees**: Scientific background, education, skills, profession, career preferences
    - **Organizations**: Company details, industry sector, hiring needs, company culture
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Job posting and discovery">
    Companies post verified job opportunities with detailed requirements, salary ranges, and benefits. Job seekers discover these through:
    
    - Advanced search with scientific field filters
    - Category browsing (biotechnology, chemistry, health, etc.)
    - Location-based filtering across Chilean cities
    - Salary range and experience level filters
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Application and tracking">
    Job seekers apply to positions directly through the platform. Both parties benefit from:
    
    - Application status tracking
    - Messaging and communication tools
    - Dashboard analytics and metrics
    - Saved jobs and favorites
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Matching and hiring">
    Companies review applications, communicate with candidates, and make hiring decisions. The platform facilitates:
    
    - Candidate comparison and evaluation
    - Interview scheduling through calendar integration
    - Transparent communication throughout the process
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The Chilean scientific sector focus

Biovity is tailored specifically for Chile's scientific job market:

- **Localized salary data** in Chilean pesos (CLP) with regional variations
- **Chilean cities and regions** as location filters
- **Local scientific companies** from biotech startups to established pharma firms
- **Spanish-first interface** with terminology familiar to Chilean professionals
- **Chilean labor standards** reflected in contract types and benefits

<Note>
  The platform uses Chilean conventions for date formats, currency (CLP), and professional terminology in the scientific sector.
</Note>

## Platform differentiators

### Specialization over generalization

While general job boards serve all industries, Biovity focuses exclusively on scientific roles. This means:

- More relevant job matches for candidates
- Better quality applicants for employers
- Industry-specific features and filters
- Content and resources tailored to science careers

### Transparency and verification

All job postings are verified to ensure:

- Legitimate companies and opportunities
- Accurate salary information
- Clear role requirements and expectations
- Professional and respectful hiring practices

### User experience for scientists

The platform is designed with scientific professionals in mind:

- Clean, professional interface without clutter
- Technical terminology and role categories scientists understand
- Filters relevant to scientific career progression
- Resources that speak to the scientific community

<Tip>
  Biovity's user experience emphasizes efficiency and clarity - values that resonate with the scientific mindset.
</Tip>

## Technology foundation

Biovity is built on modern, scalable technology:

- **Next.js 16 with App Router** for fast, SEO-friendly pages
- **React 19 and TypeScript** for type-safe, maintainable code
- **TailwindCSS 4** for responsive, accessible design
- **PostgreSQL** for reliable, relational data storage
- **Better Auth** for secure authentication and user management

This tech stack ensures Biovity can scale with Chile's growing scientific sector while maintaining performance and security.
