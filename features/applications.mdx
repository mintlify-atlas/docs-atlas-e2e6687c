Biovity provides a comprehensive application tracking system to manage your job applications from submission through interview and hiring stages.

## Application process

Applying for positions on Biovity is streamlined and integrated with your profile.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a position">
    Search for jobs using the search feature or browse recommended positions on your dashboard. Review the full job description, requirements, and responsibilities.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Apply">
    Click the "Apply" button on the job listing. Your profile information is automatically used to populate your application.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review application">
    Verify your information is complete and accurate. Add a cover letter or additional documents if required by the employer.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit application">
    Submit your application to the employer. You'll receive immediate confirmation and the application will appear in your "My Applications" section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track progress">
    Monitor your application status as it moves through the employer's review process. Receive notifications for status changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Application tracking

### Application list view

Your applications page displays:

- **Job title and company**: Quick identification of each application
- **Status badge**: Color-coded status indicator
- **Application date**: When you submitted the application
- **Last update**: Most recent status change
- **Quick actions**: View details, message recruiter, withdraw application

### Status badges

Status badges use color coding for quick scanning:

- **Blue**: Interview Scheduled - action required
- **Yellow**: Under Review - waiting for employer
- **Green**: Offer Extended - positive outcome
- **Gray**: Submitted - initial state
- **Red**: Declined - application not selected

## Application details

Click on any application to view complete details:

### Overview section

- Full job description and requirements
- Your submitted materials (resume, cover letter, documents)
- Application submission timestamp
- Current status and timeline

### Communication history

- Messages exchanged with the recruiter
- Interview scheduling details
- Status change notifications
- Any additional requests from the employer

### Interview information

When an interview is scheduled, you'll see:

- Interview date and time
- Location or video call link
- Interviewer names and roles
- Interview format (phone, video, on-site)
- Preparation materials or instructions

## Managing applications

### Responding to interview requests

When you receive an interview invitation:

1. Review the proposed date and time
2. Confirm or request an alternative time
3. Add the interview to your calendar
4. Prepare using provided materials or company information

### Withdrawing applications

If you need to withdraw an application:

1. Open the application details
2. Click "Withdraw Application"
3. Optionally provide a reason (accepted another offer, no longer interested)
4. Confirm withdrawal

The employer will be notified, and the application will be marked as withdrawn.

### Following up

Use the integrated messaging system to:

- Ask questions about the role or timeline
- Follow up on application status after reasonable waiting period
- Provide additional information requested by employer
- Thank recruiters after interviews

## Application metrics

Track your application success in the Metrics section:

- **Total applications submitted**: Overall count
- **Response rate**: Percentage of applications receiving employer response
- **Interview rate**: How many applications lead to interviews
- **Time to response**: Average employer response time
- **Application status distribution**: Visual breakdown by status
