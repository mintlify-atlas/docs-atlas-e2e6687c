## Accessing the blog

### Public blog page

All blog content is available at `/blog` route to both registered and non-registered users.

**Features:**
- Browse all published articles
- Filter by category or topic
- Search for specific content
- Share articles on social media

### Individual articles

Each article has its own dedicated page at `/blog/[slug]` with:

- Full article content with rich formatting
- Author information and publication date
- Related articles and recommendations
- Comment section (for registered users)
- Social sharing options

## Content format

Articles are published using MDX (Markdown with JSX components) for rich, interactive content:

- **Code snippets** for technical tutorials
- **Interactive charts** for data visualization
- **Embedded videos** for demonstrations
- **Image galleries** for visual content
- **Call-to-action buttons** linking to relevant jobs or resources

## Publishing schedule

New articles are published regularly:

- **Career advice**: Weekly
- **Industry insights**: Bi-weekly
- **Company spotlights**: Monthly
- **Interview guides**: As needed

## For content contributors

<Accordion title="How to submit an article">
Registered users with professional profiles can submit article proposals through the dashboard. The Biovity editorial team reviews submissions for quality and relevance before publication.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Content guidelines">
All articles must be relevant to the scientific job market, career development, or industry insights. Content should be original, well-researched, and provide value to the community.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Author attribution">
Published articles include author bylines with links to author profiles. This helps build professional credibility and visibility in the community.
</Accordion>
