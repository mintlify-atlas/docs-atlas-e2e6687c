Biovity maintains detailed company profiles to help job seekers research potential employers and make informed career decisions. All companies on the platform are verified to ensure quality and authenticity.

## Company verification

Biovity verifies all organizations before allowing them to post jobs and access the talent pool.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Company registration">
    Organizations create an account and provide company details including legal name, registration number, industry sector, and contact information.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Document verification">
    Biovity reviews company registration documents, business licenses, and other credentials to confirm legitimacy.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Profile review">
    The Biovity team reviews the company profile to ensure it meets platform standards and represents the scientific sector.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verification badge">
    Verified companies receive a verification badge displayed on their profile and job listings, giving candidates confidence in the opportunity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Workplace information

Candidates can learn about the work environment:

### Benefits and perks

Companies showcase their benefits packages:

- **Health benefits**: Medical, dental, and vision insurance coverage
- **Paid time off**: Vacation days, sick leave, and personal days
- **Professional development**: Training budgets, conference attendance, continuing education
- **Work equipment**: Laptop, lab equipment, software licenses
- **Work flexibility**: Remote work options, flexible hours, hybrid arrangements
- **Additional perks**: Gym memberships, meal allowances, transportation assistance

### Work culture

- **Team structure**: Organization chart, team sizes, collaboration style
- **Work environment**: Laboratory setup, office spaces, safety protocols
- **Innovation focus**: R&D investment, patent portfolio, publication record
- **Growth opportunities**: Career advancement paths, promotion timelines

## Finding companies

### Browse companies

Access the companies directory to explore verified employers:

- Filter by industry sector (biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, diagnostics, etc.)
- Filter by company size (startup, small, medium, large enterprise)
- Filter by location (Santiago, Valparaíso, Concepción, etc.)
- Search by company name or keywords

### Company discovery

Discover companies through:

- **Job listings**: Company name links to full profile from any job posting
- **Recommendations**: Suggested companies based on your profile and interests
- **Featured companies**: Highlighted employers in your field
- **Search results**: When browsing jobs, view the hiring company's profile

## Company job listings

Each company profile includes:

### Active positions

- All current job openings from the company
- Filterable by department, location, or experience level
- Quick apply directly from company profile

### Position characteristics

- Work modality (remote, hybrid, on-site) for each role
- Salary ranges when disclosed
- Experience levels required
- Scientific specializations needed

## Trust and safety

Biovity maintains high standards for company presence:

### Verification requirements

- Legal business registration in Chile
- Valid tax identification number
- Verified business address
- Professional email domain
- Industry relevance to scientific sector

### Ongoing monitoring

- Regular review of active companies
- Investigation of reported issues
- Removal of companies violating policies
- Update verification status as needed

### Candidate protection

If you encounter issues with a company:

- Report suspicious job postings
- Flag inappropriate communication
- Contact Biovity support for investigation
- Platform takes action on verified complaints

## Company engagement statistics

When viewing a company profile, useful metrics include:

- **Response time**: Average time to respond to applications
- **Interview rate**: Percentage of applications that lead to interviews
- **Candidate rating**: Feedback from past applicants (when available)
- **Hiring success**: Number of positions filled through Biovity
