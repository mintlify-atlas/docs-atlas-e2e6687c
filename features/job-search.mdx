Biovity's job search system helps you discover scientific positions tailored to your expertise, location preferences, and career goals. The platform focuses exclusively on opportunities in biotechnology, biochemistry, chemistry, chemical engineering, and health sciences.

## Search interface

Access the job search from your dashboard sidebar under the "Explore" section or navigate directly to the search page.

### Search components

The search interface includes:

- **Keyword search**: Search by job title, company name, or keywords
- **Advanced filters**: Refine results by multiple criteria
- **Results view**: Browse matching positions with key details
- **Quick actions**: Apply, save, or view full job details

### Work modality options

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Remote">
    Work entirely from home or any location. Ideal for roles that don't require physical lab presence.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Hybrid">
    Combination of remote work and on-site presence. Common for roles with lab work and office tasks.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="On-site">
    Full-time presence at company facilities. Typical for laboratory and production roles.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Employment formats

- **Full-time**: Standard 40-hour work week with full benefits
- **Part-time**: Reduced hours, flexible scheduling
- **Contract**: Project-based or fixed-term positions

### Experience levels

- **Junior**: Entry-level positions for recent graduates or professionals with 0-2 years experience
- **Semi-senior (Mid)**: Intermediate roles requiring 2-5 years of experience
- **Senior**: Advanced positions for experienced professionals with 5+ years

### Scientific categories

Biovity specializes in these scientific sectors:

- **Biotechnology**: Research, development, and production in biological systems
- **Biochemistry**: Molecular biology, protein chemistry, and clinical diagnostics
- **Chemistry**: Analytical chemistry, quality control, and laboratory work
- **Chemical Engineering**: Process engineering, optimization, and production
- **Food Engineering**: Food product development, safety, and quality assurance

## Job listings

Each job listing displays key information:

### Job card details

- **Job title**: Position name (e.g., "Investigador en Biotecnología")
- **Company name**: Employer organization
- **Location**: City and country (e.g., "Santiago, Chile")
- **Work modality**: Remote, hybrid, or on-site badge
- **Employment type**: Full-time, part-time, or contract
- **Salary range**: Compensation in Chilean Pesos (CLP)
- **Publication date**: When the job was posted
- **Benefits**: Health insurance, paid vacation, training budget, equipment, etc.

### Quick actions

From each job card, you can:

1. **View details**: Click to see full job description, requirements, and responsibilities
2. **Apply now**: Submit your application directly
3. **Save job**: Bookmark for later review
4. **Share**: Share opportunity with your network

## Search results

Job search results are displayed as a grid or list:

- **Relevance sorting**: Most relevant positions appear first based on your profile
- **Date sorting**: Option to view newest postings first
- **Result count**: Total number of matching positions
- **Load more**: Pagination for browsing extensive results

## Saving searches

Create job alerts to receive notifications when new positions matching your criteria are posted:

1. Configure your desired filters
2. Click "Create Alert" or "Save Search"
3. Choose notification frequency (daily, weekly)
4. Receive email alerts when matching jobs are added
