## Plan comparison

### Free plan

**Price:** $0 CLP/month (forever free)

Perfect for companies wanting to explore the platform and post their first opportunities.

**Features:**
- 5 active job postings
- Full access to candidate profiles
- Email support
- 2 featured job posts per month
- Complete ATS (Applicant Tracking System)

**Best for:** Startups and small companies testing the platform

---

### Pro plan

**Price:** $40,000 CLP/month

Ideal for companies actively recruiting in the scientific sector.

**Features:**
- 10 active job postings
- Full access to candidate profiles
- Advanced search filters
- 4 featured job posts per month
- Email and phone support
- Complete ATS
- **AI Matching** - Intelligent candidate recommendations

**Best for:** Growing companies with regular hiring needs

## Feature breakdown

### AI Matching

Available on Pro, Business, and Enterprise plans, AI Matching analyzes your job requirements and recommends candidates who best match based on:

- Relevant experience in scientific fields
- Technical skills and certifications
- Career preferences and goals
- Location and work modality preferences

### Applicant Tracking System (ATS)

All plans include Biovity's complete ATS for managing the full recruitment lifecycle:

- Publish and manage job postings
- Receive and organize applications
- Filter and search candidate profiles
- Schedule interviews
- Communicate directly with candidates
- Track application status and hiring pipeline

### Featured job posts

Boost visibility of your most important positions with featured placements:

- Appear at the top of job search results
- Highlighted badge for better visibility
- Increased candidate views and applications
- Strategic placement on homepage

## Upgrading and downgrading

You can change your plan at any time from your organization dashboard:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access billing settings">
    Navigate to **Settings** > **Billing & Plans** in your organization dashboard
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose new plan">
    Review available plans and select the one that fits your needs
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm change">
    Changes apply immediately with prorated billing for remaining days in your cycle
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
When downgrading, ensure your active job postings don't exceed the new plan's limit. You may need to deactivate some postings before downgrading.
</Warning>

## Payment methods

Biovity accepts the following payment methods:

- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Bank transfer
- Webpay (Chilean payment system)
- Monthly invoicing (Enterprise plans)

## Cancellation policy

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

1. Your account remains active until the end of the current billing period
2. No refunds for partial months
3. You can reactivate your subscription anytime
4. Your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation

## Special programs

### Startup discount

Early-stage startups may qualify for special pricing. Requirements:

- Founded within the last 2 years
- Fewer than 10 employees
- Chilean-registered company

### Academic institutions

Universities and research institutions can access special pricing. Contact our team for academic rates.

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordion title="Can I try Biovity for free?">
Yes! The Free plan is available forever with no credit card required. You get 5 active job postings and access to candidate profiles to explore the platform.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change plans anytime?">
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes apply immediately with prorated billing based on remaining days in your billing cycle.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I need more job postings?">
You can upgrade to a higher plan or contact us for custom Enterprise pricing if you need more than 20 active postings.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a contract commitment?">
No long-term contracts required for Free, Pro, and Business plans. You can cancel anytime. Enterprise plans may include annual agreements depending on custom terms.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does AI Matching work?">
AI Matching uses machine learning to analyze job requirements and candidate profiles, suggesting the best matches based on experience, skills, location, and preferences. Available on Pro plans and above.
</Accordion>
