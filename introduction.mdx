# Welcome to Biovity

Biovity is a Chilean SaaS job platform designed to bridge the gap between scientific talent and opportunities in the biotechnology, biochemistry, chemistry, chemical engineering, and health sectors.

<Note>
  Built with cutting-edge technology: Next.js 16, React 19, TypeScript, TailwindCSS 4, PostgreSQL, and Better Auth.
</Note>

## What makes Biovity different?

Biovity specializes exclusively in the scientific sector, providing a focused platform where:

- **Professionals and students** find relevant opportunities in their specialized fields
- **Scientific companies** connect with qualified talent in biotechnology and related sciences
- **Transparent processes** ensure verified job offers and authentic opportunities
- **Chilean market focus** with localized features and salary information in CLP

## Platform overview

<Steps>
  <Step title="Job seekers register">
    Professionals and students create profiles with their scientific background and career preferences
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Companies post opportunities">
    Organizations publish verified job offers tailored to the scientific sector
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Smart matching">
    The platform connects talent with relevant opportunities through advanced search and filtering
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply and track">
    Users apply to positions and track their application status through the dashboard
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Biovity serves both job seekers (employees) and organizations with separate dashboards and workflows optimized for each user type.
</Tip>
